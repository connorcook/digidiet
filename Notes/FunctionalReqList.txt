List of Functional Requirements

1) Users have ability to search for recipes
2) Users have ability to rate recipes out of 5 stars
3) Users have ability to write a comment or review of recipe
4) Users have ability to flag a comment for review
5) Moderators can review and approve content
6) Administrators can designate and remove moderators
7) Administrators can change the front page
8) Administrators can ban users
9) Users will be notified if content is taken down
